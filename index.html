A little about me...

I'm a history major, a lifelong learner and a bit of a polymath. Professionally, I've led teams, both large and small, designed systems to improve efficiency for a nationwide chain, and even developed delicious food menus.

Bootcamper

After 11 years in food service I took a sharp turn to pursue web development through Awesome Inc's bootcamp. Over the past several months, I have pushed myself inside and outside the classroom to gain valuable skills to be an asset as a developer.

Tinkerer

As a lifelong learner, if I'm not taking things apart, I'm usually putting things together. "I wonder what would happen if..." is common origin of most of my projects. I've built a coffee roaster from Goodwill appliances, and I'm currently learning to garden and build costumes for my kids.

Team Player and Leader

It's a cliche that 'teamwork makes the dream work' but I've found that to be true leading groups regardless of group size. I believe in the power of collective success and giving everyone an opportunity to provide value in their work.
